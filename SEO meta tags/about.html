About Simply Charmlings

Simply Charmlings brings to you handcrafted polymer clay charmlings that are waiting to be adopted. Hang them on your bracelet, necklace, bags, or wherever, because they are content as long as they’re with you. Because of their handcrafted nature, each charmlings are unique and no two are alike.
